Our Choir Ministry

  • Welcome to our church family
  • We sing praises and worship God together
  • We also preach the Word of God
  • Our pastors prepare us to be ministers of God's word

What We Do

  • Worship Services: Lead congregation in praise and worship
  • Special Events: Perform at church celebrations and ceremonies
  • Community Outreach: Share God's love through music
  • Fellowship: Build lasting friendships in Christ

Why Join Our Choir?

Spiritual Growth

Deepen your relationship with God through worship and service. Our choir members grow spiritually as they minister to others through music.

Community

Join a loving family of believers who support and encourage one another. Form lasting friendships with fellow choir members.

Ministry

Use your musical gifts to serve God and bless others. Be part of creating an atmosphere of worship and praise.

How to Join

Interested in joining our choir ministry? Here's how you can get started:

  1. Attend a Service: Come visit us on Sunday to experience our worship firsthand
  2. Speak with Our Music Director: Express your interest in joining the choir
  3. Attend Practice: Join our weekly choir practice sessions
  4. Become Part of the Family: Start ministering with us in worship services

No prior experience necessary - just a heart for worship and a desire to serve God!
