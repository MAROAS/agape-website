# AGAPE Church Website

## Welcome to AGAPE Church Queensland, Australia

A modern, responsive website for AGAPE Church located in Brisbane, Queensland, Australia.

## Website Overview

This website serves as the digital home for AGAPE Church, providing information about our services, ministries, and community. We are a Christ-centered congregation that welcomes everyone to experience God's love and grace.

## Pages

### Home (`home.html`)
- **Purpose**: Main landing page with overview of church services and ministries
- **Features**: Welcome message, quick access to key information, ministry highlights
- **Call-to-Action**: Learn more about the church, view service times, contact information

### About Us (`ministry.html`)
- **Purpose**: Detailed information about the church's mission, vision, and ministries
- **Features**: Church history, what to expect when visiting, ministry descriptions
- **Call-to-Action**: Plan a visit, learn about ministries

### Pastors (`pastors.html`)
- **Purpose**: Information about church leadership and pastoral team
- **Features**: Pastor profiles, ministry focus, spiritual guidance services
- **Call-to-Action**: Meet the senior pastor, learn about pastoral care

### Pastor Contact (`pastor.html`)
- **Purpose**: Dedicated page for senior pastor and contact information
- **Features**: Pastor David Mwenda Ulieli's profile, contact details, church location
- **Call-to-Action**: Contact the pastor, plan a visit

### Choir Ministry (`choir.html`)
- **Purpose**: Information about the church choir and music ministry
- **Features**: Choir activities, how to join, worship through music
- **Call-to-Action**: Join the choir, participate in worship ministry

### Service Times (`time.html`)
- **Purpose**: Visitor information and service details
- **Features**: What to expect, Sunday activities, visitor welcome
- **Call-to-Action**: Plan your visit, contact for more information

## Design Principles

### Color Palette
- **Primary Green**: #1f603e (church branding)
- **Secondary Green**: #2c5530
- **Accent Green**: #4CAF50
- **Gold/Yellow**: #fdb500 (highlights)
- **White/Light Gray**: #fafafa (backgrounds)

### Typography
- **Headers**: Georgia, Times New Roman (serif)
- **Body**: Georgia, Times New Roman (serif)
- **Hierarchy**: Clear font sizes from 2.5em (h1) to 1.1em (h5/h6)

### Layout Principles
- **Container Width**: Maximum 1200px centered
- **Spacing**: Consistent rem units (1rem, 2rem, 3rem)
- **Grid**: Flexible CSS Grid for responsive layouts
- **Cards**: White backgrounds with subtle shadows
