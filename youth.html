What We Do

Worship Nights

Experience powerful worship, prayer, and teaching that will inspire and strengthen your faith.

Small Groups

Hang out with friends, dive into the Bible, and talk about real life in a safe, welcoming space.

Community Impact

Serve together—helping our city, supporting families, and showing God’s love in action.

Why Join ACY?

  • Make lifelong friends who share your faith journey
  • Discover your gifts and how to use them for God’s glory
  • Grow in confidence, purpose, and leadership
  • Have fun at camps, sports, and creative events

A Message of Hope

Life can feel overwhelming, but you don’t have to walk alone. At AC Youth, we believe Jesus brings joy, peace, and purpose. Come as you are—you’re welcome here!
